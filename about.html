About Us

Welcome to our organization! We are dedicated to creating positive change in our communities through impactful initiatives. Our mission is to bring people together to make a difference and support those in need. With a vision for a brighter and more inclusive future, we strive to empower individuals and transform lives.

Mitchell Okpu

Mitchell is a seasoned business and finance professional with over a decade of experience in the global renewable industry. Joyce Daniels

Joyce Daniels, known as the "Queen of Talk," is a renowned Nigerian Event Host MC, Public Speaking Coach, and Thought Leader with over a decade of experience. She has trained over 18,000 individuals in speaking and MCing, authored two books, and is celebrated for shaping the speaking and MC business in Nigeria. As a mindset coach, Joyce empowers individuals to build confidence, resilience, and efficient systems for personal growth. An advocate for inclusive politics in Africa, she is affiliated with WIMBIZ, WISCAR, and WEOG, and reaches over 30,000 readers weekly with her insights.

Tariere Egbegi

Tariere Egbegi, Esq, is a dedicated legal professional with a passion for service and excellence. A graduate of Rivers State University with an LL.B and a master's degree in Oil and Gas Law from Robert Gordon University, Scotland, she was called to the Nigerian Bar in 2003. With extensive leadership experience, she has held roles such as Chairperson of FIDA Nigeria (Bayelsa State), Vice Chair of the NBA Yenagoa Branch, and Coordinator of 100 Women Lobby Group, among others. Currently serving as an Assistant Director in the Ministry of Justice, Yenagoa, her expertise spans legal practice, alternative dispute resolution, and advocacy. Tariere is a team player who enjoys research, reading, and cultural exploration, equipping her to excel in diverse roles.
